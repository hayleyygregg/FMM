My Story

I am currently studying Interaction Design, an awesome new course at the Belfast School of Art. With experience in a previous course of hardcore coding, I knew it wasn’t what I was passionate about. A strong believer in “do what you love” I took a leap of faith to follow my love for all things design and it was the best decision I ever made!

Discovering Typography and inspired by Hand-Drawn Type, I knew it is what I was born to do. Therefore my mission is to make the world beautiful with Typography, one letter at a time.

Things that interest me include Branding, creating logos, coming up with ideas and collaborating with a team to make great things! When I’m not sketching or fiddling with anchor points on Illustrator on a daily basis, I like to get my daily dose of exercise as it clears my head and gives me a buzz! A deal breaker might be that I haven’t seen Breaking Bad, but I do have an addiction to Friends — everybody loves Friends.

Why Me?

I'm a student willing to give up my summer for work, if that doesn’t show dedication I don't know what will! I'm not your typical ‘party-animal’ student as I have always been described as conscientious and hard working. I have the personality of a perfectionist, paying close attention to detail, so you can count on me to give 100% effort in any task.

I’d love to have the opportunity to work for Fresh Made Media as it would give me experience of working in the ‘real-life’ industry and allow me to develop and expand my skillset, making fresh-friends along the way!

The real question is, am I Fresh Made Material?
