About Me

I am currently studying Interaction Design, an awesome new course at the University of Ulster in Belfast. With experience in developing webpages in a previous course, I knew it wasn't what I was passionate about. A strong believer in “Do what you love” I took a leap of faith to follow my love for all things design and it was the best decision I ever made!

Discovering Typography and inspired by Hand-Drawn Type, I knew it is what I was born to do. Therefore my mission is to make the world beautiful with Typography, one letter at a time.
